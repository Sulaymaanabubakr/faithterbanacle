Welcome to Faith Tabernacle

Faith Tabernacle is a vibrant and dynamic faith assembly under the Living Faith Church Worldwide (also known as Winners' Chapel International). We are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission by teaching God's Word, winning souls for Christ, and transforming lives through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our church is a place where believers are equipped to live victoriously, serve passionately, and impact their world positively. We believe in the integrity of God's Word and the manifestation of His promises in the lives of all who believe.

Since our establishment, we have been a beacon of hope and light, providing spiritual guidance, fostering fellowship, and creating an atmosphere where God's presence is tangible and transformative.

Our Mission & Vision

What drives us forward

Our Mission

To teach the unadulterated Word of God in its simplicity and power, thereby making disciples of all nations, bringing believers to a place of spiritual maturity, and creating an atmosphere for fellowship and the manifestation of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

To raise a people who are totally dependent on God's Word for all their needs, fulfilling God's purpose for their lives, and impacting their world through the gospel of Jesus Christ, demonstrating love, excellence, and integrity in all they do.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide us

Word-Based

We are founded on the uncompromising integrity of God's Word, believing in its power to transform lives and situations.

Love

We demonstrate genuine love for God and for one another, creating a welcoming and caring community.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in all we do, giving God our best in worship, service, and daily living.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and moral uprightness in all our dealings.

Fellowship

We foster strong relationships and community, encouraging believers to grow together in faith.

Evangelism

We are passionate about reaching the lost with the gospel and making disciples of all nations.
