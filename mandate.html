Our Divine Mandate

The commission God has given us

To Liberate the World Through the Word

Faith Tabernacle is founded on the divine mandate to liberate people from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the word of faith. This mandate is derived from our Lord Jesus Christ's commission to His disciples.

We are called to take the gospel to every nation, making disciples and teaching them to observe all things commanded by Christ. This mandate drives everything we do, from our worship services to our outreach programs.

Through the power of God's word, we have witnessed countless lives transformed, families restored, and communities impacted. This is not just our missionâ€”it is our divine calling and purpose.

Our Biblical Foundation

Matthew 28:19-20

"Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, teaching them to observe all things I have commanded you."

Mark 16:15

"Go into all the world and preach the gospel to every creature."

Luke 4:18

"The Spirit of the Lord is upon Me, because He has anointed Me to preach the gospel to the poor; He has sent Me to heal the brokenhearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives."

Pillars of Our Mandate

The foundational elements of our calling

Evangelism

Taking the gospel to the unreached, winning souls for Christ, and expanding the Kingdom of God on earth.

Discipleship

Building believers through systematic teaching of God's word and raising spiritual champions.

Service

Demonstrating Christ's love through practical acts of service and compassion to our community.

Global Impact

Extending our reach to nations, cultures, and peoples across the world with the message of hope.
