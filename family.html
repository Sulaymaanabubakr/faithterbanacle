Our Family Ministry

The Christian Family Ministry at Faith Tabernacle is dedicated to strengthening marriages, nurturing children, and building godly homes based on biblical principles.

We believe that strong families are the foundation of a healthy church and society. Through our programs, we provide practical tools, biblical teaching, and supportive community for families at every stage.

Join us in creating homes that reflect God's love, grace, and purpose.
