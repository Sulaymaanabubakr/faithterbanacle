Who We Are

Faith Tabernacle is a Bible-believing church and a faith assembly under Living Faith Church Worldwide (also known as Winners' Chapel). We are committed to the proclamation of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ and the word of faith to the nations of the world.

Founded on the vision to liberate people from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the word of faith, we have grown into a vibrant community of believers dedicated to worship, fellowship, and service.

Our church is built on the solid foundation of God's word, and we are passionate about seeing lives transformed through the power of the gospel.

Our Mission

To spread the gospel of Jesus Christ to all nations, making disciples and building believers through the teaching of God's word. We are committed to demonstrating the love of Christ through worship, service, and fellowship.

Our Vision

To become a model church that impacts our community and the world, raising champions of faith who will transform their generation through the power of God's word and the demonstration of His Spirit.
