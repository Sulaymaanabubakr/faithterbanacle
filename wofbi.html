About WOFBI

Building solid biblical foundation for effective ministry

Equipping Believers for Ministry

Word of Faith Bible Institute (WOFBI) is our Bible training school designed to equip believers with a solid foundation in God's word and practical ministry skills.

Through systematic study of the Scriptures, students gain deep understanding of biblical principles, doctrines, and practical application for daily Christian living and ministry effectiveness.

Our curriculum covers essential topics including Biblical Studies, Christian Doctrine, Church History, Practical Ministry, and Leadership Development.

Programs Offered

Certificate Program

One-year intensive study covering fundamental biblical doctrines and principles.

Diploma Program

Two-year comprehensive study with practical ministry training and internship.

Advanced Studies

Specialized courses for ministry leaders and advanced theological training.

Why Choose WOFBI?

Experienced Faculty

Learn from seasoned ministers and Bible scholars with years of practical ministry experience.

Flexible Schedule

Classes designed to accommodate working adults with evening and weekend options.
