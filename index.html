Welcome to Faith Tabernacle

We are a Bible-believing church committed to the proclamation of the gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ and the word of faith to the nations of the world.

Faith Tabernacle is a vibrant assembly under Living Faith Church Worldwide, founded on the mandate to liberate people from all oppressions of the devil through the preaching of the word of faith.

Join us as we discover God's purpose and destiny for our lives through inspiring worship, powerful teaching, and genuine fellowship.
